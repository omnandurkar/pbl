JSPM Group Of Institutes

In Pune, The JSPM group has some of the top-rated engineering colleges. Moreover, they have some renowned colleges that offer courses in Business Administration, Computer Application, Pharmacy, Liberal Arts, Science & Commerce.They have established a proven education system in all of their campuses, which is characterized by well, thought out curriculum and effective yet out of the box teaching methodologies. What makes them to stand out is an effective collation of leadership strategies and vibrant education that renders unassailable competitive advantage ensuring a seamless growth across all verticals. They are duly committed to provide students with a learning experience that will prepare them to take on the competitive challenges of professional world and emerge victorious in all aspects of life. In this endeavour, they have taken meticulous care to set quality standards, which are at par with the best universities in the world.

The JSPM University Act has been issued on 6th January 2023 and the Department of Higher and Technical Education has issued the notification for the establishment of University from 16th January 2023. the Government of Maharashtra appoints the 28th February, 2023 to be the date on which JSPM University, Pune shall become operational. The University shall be in operation from the Academic year 2023-24. The University shall offer academic programs through the school of Science & Engineering, School of Commerce &Management, School of Pharmaceutical Sciences,School of Liberal Arts and School of Skills & Professional Development

As an elite institute that offers quality education in various disciplines of engineering, management, pharmacy they care for the well-being of students and has set up the best hostel facility for boys & girls where they will be safe and pursue their education. The hostel takes due care of all its boarders and ensures that they have a comfortable and safe stay. Every student is provided with sufficient amenities of dining and lodging. In addition, the hostel premises are WIFI enabled and students can maintain good connectivity with the outside world.
